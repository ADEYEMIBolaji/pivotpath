How it works

Five steps from stuck to in motion

One honest pass over your background. Every step ends with a clear next action — never a dead end.

01
Ingest

Drop in your résumé or paste a LinkedIn profile. We extract titles, tenures and accomplishments — messy formatting and non-linear histories included.

02
Translate

Every line is mapped to the vocabulary, frameworks and action verbs your target field actually uses. You see your old work in the new language, side by side.

03
Score

An honest Pivot Readiness Score, with a confidence band — never false precision. Disqualifying gaps, closable gaps and noise are clearly separated.

04
Rewrite

A repositioned résumé in the new field's language. Every bullet is traceable to something true — a reframe of real experience, not embellishment.

05
Apply

Where to apply, which bridge roles to target first, and a 30-day plan. You never leave a screen without a clear next action.
